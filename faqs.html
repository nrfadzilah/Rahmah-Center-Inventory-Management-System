Frequently Asked Questions

Rahmah Center FAQ

What is the Rahmah Center Inventory Management System?

The Rahmah Center Inventory Management System is a web application that helps manage the stock of basic necessities available at the Rahmah Center. It allows users to check item availability and allows admins to track, manage, and restock inventory efficiently.

Who can use the inventory management system?

The system is designed for both students and administrators. Students can view available items and track items they take, while administrators have access to inventory management tools, such as adding or updating stock levels and reviewing item usage.

Do I need an account to use the system?

Only administrators need an account to log in and manage inventory. Students can access certain parts of the system without a login to check item availability and track what item they take.

How can I check the availability of items in stock?

Students can view available items directly from the main inventory page without logging in. This page shows the current stock levels of essential items.

How do admins restock items?

Admins can access the admin dashboard after logging in. From there, they can view current stock levels, add new items, or update quantities to restock the items as needed.

How is the data on item usage tracked?

The system will generate a receipt for the items that a student takes. Admins will then deduct the corresponding quantities from the stock based on the items listed in the receipt.

Who can I contact if I have additional questions or need support?

If you have further questions, please reach out to the Rahmah Center support team or the designated administrator. They can help with any inquiries related to the system.
